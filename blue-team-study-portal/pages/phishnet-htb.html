PhishNet (HTB)

Scenario

An accounting team receives an urgent payment request from a known vendor. The email appears legitimate but contains a suspicious link and a .zip attachment hiding malware. Your task is to analyze the email headers, and uncover the attacker's scheme.

https://app.hackthebox.com/sherlocks/PhishNet

For a quick win, we can use the eioc.py tool: https://github.com/MalwareCube/Email-IOC-Extractor

1- What is the originating IP address of the sender?

45.67.89.10

2- Which mail server relayed this email before reaching the victim?

203.0.113.25

Each mail transfer agent (MTA) that accepts the message normally prepends a Received: header to the top of the header block. That means the topmost Received: is the last hop (closest to the recipient), and the bottommost Received: is the earliest hop you can see (closest to the origin).

3- What is the sender's email address?

finance@business-finance.com

4- What is the 'Reply-To' email address specified in the email?

support@business-finance.com

5- What is the SPF (Sender Policy Framework) result for this email?

pass

The SPF check passed because the sending IP is authorized by the domain. This can occur when a legitimate domain or mailbox is compromised or when an allowed third-party service is abused, so a PASS does not rule out malicious content.

OR If the attacker controls the sending domain, they can set SPF to authorize their mail servers, so SPF alone does not prove the message is legitimate.

6- What is the domain used in the phishing URL inside the email?

secure.business-finance.com

7- What is the fake company name used in the email?

Business Finance Ltd.

8- What is the name of the attachment included in the email?

Invoice_2025_Payment.zip

9- What is the SHA-256 hash of the attachment?

8379C41239E9AF845B2AB6C27A7509AE8804D7D73E455C800A551B22BA25BB4A

10- What is the filename of the malicious file contained within the ZIP attachment?

invoice_document.pdf.bat

11- Which MITRE ATT&CK techniques are associated with this attack?

T1566.001
