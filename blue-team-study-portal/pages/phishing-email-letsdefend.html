Phishing Email (LetsDefend)

Scenario

Your email address has been leaked and you receive an email from Paypal in German. Try to analyze the suspicious email.

https://app.letsdefend.io/challenge/phishing-email

1- What is the return path of the email?

bounce@rjttznyzjjzydnillquh.designclub.uk.com

from the email we can see its trying to trick the user telling him that he won a prize from paypal

(it was written in German if you are wondering)

looking at the the From header and Return-Path this is very sus

2- What is the domain name of the url in this mail?

storage.googleapis.com

The phishing email includes a link to http://storage.googleapis.com/, which is part of Google Cloud Storage. Attackers commonly abuse trusted cloud services like this to host malicious payloads (e.g., ZIP files, fake documents, or phishing pages).

3- Is the domain mentioned in the previous question suspicious?

yes

4- What is the body SHA-256 of the domain?

13945ecc33afee74ac7f72e1d5bb73050894356c4bf63d02a1a53e76830567f5

5- Is this email a phishing email?

yes
