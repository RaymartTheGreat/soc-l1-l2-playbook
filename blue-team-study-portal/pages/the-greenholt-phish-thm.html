The Greenholt Phish (THM)

Scenario

A Sales Executive at Greenholt PLC received an email that he didn't expect to receive from a customer. He claims that the customer never uses generic greetings such as "Good day" and didn't expect any amount of money to be transferred to his account. The email also contains an attachment that he never requested. He forwarded the email to the SOC (Security Operations Center) department for further investigation.

Investigate the email sample to determine if it is legitimate.

https://tryhackme.com/room/phishingemails5fgjlzxc

1- What is the Transfer Reference Number listed in the email's Subject?

09674321

can see that the subject contains Reference Number and the body have it as well

2- Who is the email from?

Mr. James Jackson

can see the email was sent form Mr james as shown in the From and Replay to but we see that the email in the replay is different from the from

3- What is his email address?

info@mutawamarine.com

4- What email address will receive a reply to this email?

info.mutawamarine@mail.com

5- What is the Originating IP?

192.119.71.157

we can see that bottom Received header is the first hop that received this email which it contains the domain and ip of the sender

6- Who is the owner of the Originating IP? (Do not include the "." in your answer.)

Hostwinds LLC

https://www.whois.com/whois/192.119.71.157

using whois can find the Organization who owns the ip

7- What is the SPF record for the Return-Path domain?

v=spf1 include:spf.protection.outlook.com -all

copying the source to mxtoolbox.com will see that it will analyze SPF and DMARC for us

we can see that spf.protection.outlook.com is the authorized sender if the receiver didn’t receive from this sender it will tell the receiver to reject it

8- What is the DMARC record for the Return-Path domain?

v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; fo=1

this DMARC record tells us that any emails that fails both SPF and DKIM will be flagged and sent to the spam/junk folder and generate reports for failed SPF/DKIM

9- What is the name of the attachment?

SWT_#09674321____PDF__.CAB

10- What is the SHA256 hash of the file attachment?

2e91c533615a9bb8929ac4bb76707b2444597ce063d84a4b33525e25074fff3f

will have to save this file then sha265sum

11- What is the attachments file size? (Don't forget to add "KB" to your answer, NUM KB)

400.26 KB

we can search VirusTotal with the hash and get the size

12- What is the actual file extension of the attachment?

RAR

VirusTotal also reveals this file attachment is RAR file
