Original Notes

Introduction

Cyber Kill Chain is a strategic framework developed by Lockheed Martin to model the structured progression of cyberattacks. It breaks down a typical attack into distinct stages, from initial reconnaissance to achieving the attacker's final objective.

This model helps cybersecurity professionals by:

  • Detecting threats earlier in the attack lifecycle.
  • Disrupting attacker activity at key points.
  • Optimizing defenses and allocating resources using informed threat intelligence.

By understanding each stage of the Cyber Kill Chain, defenders can anticipate attacker behavior and implement targeted controls to reduce risk and damage.
