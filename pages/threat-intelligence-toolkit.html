Threat Intelligence Toolkit

Threat Intelligence Toolkit (Organized)

MITRE ATT&amp;CK

A knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques based on real-world observations, used for understanding and detecting cyber threats.

VirusTotal

A tool that analyzes files and URLs for viruses, malware, and other suspicious content by using multiple antivirus engines and tools.

ThreatCrowd

A platform for searching and analyzing threat intelligence data, including IPs, domains, and malware hashes associated with cyber attacks.

MISP (Malware Information Sharing Platform &amp; Threat Sharing)

An open-source threat intelligence platform designed for collecting, storing, and sharing structured threat information such as indicators of compromise (IOCs), TTPs, and threat actor profiles, enabling collaboration among organizations and security teams.

ThreatConnect

A threat intelligence platform that combines threat data aggregation, analytics, and orchestration to help security teams manage, correlate, and act on threat intelligence within a single operational workflow.

APT_REPORT

A curated collection of APT (Advanced Persistent Threat) reports and indicators, organized by threat group and region. Useful for studying attacker behaviors, malware families, and techniques used in real-world cyber espionage campaigns.

Anomali

A threat intelligence platform that delivers contextual threat insights by aggregating global threat feeds, matching them against internal telemetry, and integrating with security tools for proactive threat detection and response.

ThreatQ

A threat intelligence platform built for threat operations and management, enabling prioritization and enrichment of threat data, collaborative investigation, and integration into detection and response workflows across security tools.

ANY.RUN

An interactive malware analysis tool that allows users to analyze suspicious files in a virtual environment and observe their behavior.

Joe Security

A platform providing malware analysis, focusing on dynamic analysis and providing reports to understand the behavior of malware.

Hybrid Analysis

An advanced malware analysis platform that allows users to submit files for analysis in a sandbox and provides detailed reports on the file's behavior.

IP Geolocation

A service that provides geolocation information for IP addresses, including country, city, and other data, useful for tracking threat actors.

Shodan

A search engine for internet-connected devices that allows you to discover and analyze vulnerable systems and services exposed online.

ThreatMiner

A threat intelligence platform that provides insight into various types of indicators of compromise (IoCs), including malware samples, IPs, and domains.

Reverse DNS Lookup

A service for performing reverse DNS lookups to identify domain names associated with IP addresses.

MXToolbox

A tool that helps with checking DNS records, domain health, and email server configurations to diagnose network issues and improve security.

URLScan

A service for scanning and analyzing URLs to detect malicious activities and provide insights into web page content and behavior.

Have I Been Pwned

A service for checking whether your email or other personal data has been exposed in a data breach.

Dehashed

A breach data search engine that allows you to search across billions of records for breached information, including email addresses, usernames, and passwords.

CyberChef

An open-source tool for performing a wide variety of operations on data, such as encoding, encryption, and decoding, with an easy-to-use interface.

Abuse.ch

A project providing free access to malware threat intelligence data and various services like malware hashes, URLs, and IPs associated with abuse.

PhishTank

A community-driven service for submitting and verifying phishing websites to help protect users from fraudulent sites.

URLHaus

A platform that provides a collection of malicious URLs, frequently updated, to help in detecting and blocking phishing and malware sites.

Talos Intelligence

Cisco's threat intelligence team provides security research, including malware analysis, vulnerability assessments, and other security tools and reports.

Open Threat Exchange (OTX)

A platform for sharing threat intelligence and collaborating on detecting and mitigating cyber threats.

CrackStation

A tool for cracking hashed passwords using large precomputed hash databases, useful for penetration testing and security research.

MITRE D3FEND

A knowledge base of cybersecurity defense techniques, providing a comprehensive framework to help organizations protect against various cyber threats. It maps defensive strategies to MITRE ATT&CK and focuses on helping defenders mitigate attacks.

Gary Kessler's File Signatures

A resource for identifying and comparing file signatures used for identifying files based on their binary contents.

CRT.sh

A public Certificate Transparency log search engine that allows users to search for SSL/TLS certificates issued by Certificate Authorities (CAs).

Internet Archive (Wayback Machine)

A digital archive that allows users to view archived versions of websites, enabling the retrieval of past content from the internet.

EchoTrail

A platform providing a collection of high-fidelity threat intelligence, aimed at identifying and tracking advanced persistent threats (APTs) and other cyber threat activities through open-source and commercial data sources.

LOLBAS Project

A curated collection of living off the land binaries and scripts (LOLBAS) that can be used by attackers for malicious purposes, leveraging legitimate system tools to evade detection.

The DFIR Report

A website dedicated to providing in-depth, real-world analysis of digital forensics and incident response (DFIR) cases, offering insights into various cybersecurity incidents and helping professionals improve detection and response strategies.

Robtex

A tool for gathering intelligence on IP addresses, domains, and networks by analyzing DNS, routing, and other internet-related data.

WHOIS

A lookup service that retrieves domain registration details, including ownership, expiration dates, and contact information.

Uncoder.io

A tool for converting queries between different SIEM formats, helping analysts translate search queries across platforms like Splunk, ELK, and Sentinel.

Browserling

An online cross-browser testing tool that allows users to test websites in real browsers without needing local installations.

IBM X-Force Exchange

A threat intelligence sharing platform that provides insights on cybersecurity threats, including malware analysis, threat indicators, and security research.

BlacklistMaster

A comprehensive blacklist monitoring service that checks IPv4, IPv6 addresses and domains against over 90 real-time blocklists (RBLs). Features include customizable monitoring intervals (from 20 minutes to 24 hours), forward-confirmed reverse DNS tests on every check, severity scoring, and multi-channel notifications (Telegram, Slack, Discord, webhooks). It offers bulk IP/range management, phishing alerts via PhishTank and URLhaus integrations, advanced drill‑down reporting, and a synchronous API for seamless integration into existing security workflows.

Snusbase

A breach aggregation and monitoring platform that collects leak and paste data from forums, dark web marketplaces, and public sources, offering search, bulk lookup, and alerting services for emails, usernames, and other exposed information.

MalwareBazaar

A community-driven platform operated by abuse.ch that facilitates the sharing and analysis of malware samples. MalwareBazaar enables security researchers and analysts to upload, search, and download malware samples to support threat intelligence, detection rule development, and reverse engineering efforts. It offers enrichment via YARA rules, AV detection, tags, and correlations with other intelligence platforms like VirusTotal and MISP.

ThreatFox

A free platform by abuse.ch for sharing indicators of compromise (IOCs) related to malware and botnets. It allows users to contribute and search IOCs like IPs, domains, and URLs, enabling rapid detection and response to emerging threats.

IntelX (Intelligence X)

A powerful search engine and data archive for digital investigations, indexing darknet content, leaked data, WHOIS, and public records. Commonly used by security professionals and researchers for OSINT, breach hunting, and threat attribution.

Triage

A cloud-based sandbox analysis platform developed by Hatching for detonation of malware in simulated environments. Provides automated behavioral analysis, memory dumps, MITRE ATT&CK mapping, and enriched indicators for reverse engineering and threat intel.

CAPEv2

A community-driven malware sandboxing framework designed for unpacking and analyzing malware, especially droppers and fileless threats. Built on Cuckoo, CAPEv2 specializes in extracting configurations, payloads, and behavioral data for in-depth analysis.

Malpedia

An open knowledge base of malware families, maintained by the Fraunhofer Institute. Malpedia provides structured YARA rules, technical profiles, and relationships between malware, threat actors, and campaigns, supporting both threat hunting and reverse engineering.

BinaryEdge

A threat intelligence and attack surface monitoring platform that scans internet-facing assets globally. It provides real-time data on exposed services, vulnerabilities, and risky infrastructure, helping defenders identify weak points and suspicious behavior.

SecurityTrails

A comprehensive platform offering DNS, WHOIS, IP, and historical infrastructure intelligence. SecurityTrails enables deep reconnaissance of internet assets, domain relationships, and attack surface data — useful for red teaming and threat actor tracking.

AbuseIPDB

A community-powered database that tracks malicious IP addresses engaged in activities like brute force, spamming, and port scanning. Users can report and check abuse history to help block hostile IPs and reduce network threats.

IOC Bucket

An open-source repository of threat intelligence containing structured IOCs such as hashes, URLs, domains, and IPs. It aggregates data from multiple community feeds, supporting both manual threat hunting and automated ingestion via its API.
