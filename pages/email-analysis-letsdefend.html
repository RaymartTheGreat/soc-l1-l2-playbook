Email Analysis (LetsDefend)

Scenario

You recently received an email from someone trying to impersonate a company, your job is to analyze the email to see if it is suspicious.

https://app.letsdefend.io/challenge/email-analysis

by just looking at the email we can see a spoofed domain with urgency for payments and luring to open the attachment

1- What is the sending email address?

yanting@united.com.sg

From header

2- What is the email address of the recipient?

admin@malware-traffic-analysis.net

To header

3- What is the subject line of the email?

united scientific equipment

Subject header

4- What date was the Email sent? Date format: MM/DD/YYYY

02/08/2021

Date header

5- What is the originating IP?

71.19.248.52

Received header or X-Originating-IP header

6- What country is the ip address from?

Canada

https://ipgeolocation.io/what-is-my-ip/71.19.248.52

7- What is the name of the attachment when you unzip it? (with extension)

united scientific equipent.exe

just unzip the file and copy the name

8- What is the sha256 hash of the File?

9909753BFB0AC8AB165BAB3555233D03B01A9274A92E57C022F87CCBE51CA415

open powershell and use Get-FileHash cmdlet to get the hash

9- Is the email attachment malicious? Yes/No

Yes
